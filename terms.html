Hierarchy of the Organisation ChES, Chemical Engineering Society is a student chapter of the chemical engineering department of SVNIT that was instituted on 16th January 2104. Affiliated to the American Institute of Chemical Engineering (AIChE), ChES aims of spreading knowledge and experience to the future chemical engineers about chemical engineering education and application. Dr. Meghal Desai is currently the faculty advisor of the student chapter. The society comprises of five core committee members, the President, Vice-President, Managerial secretary, Technical Secretary and the Treasurer. The President is responsible for all the decisions and actions made by the committee and also heads the core committee. The Vice-President is the second highest in Hierarchy and plays a major role in the decision-making of the committee. Managerial Secretary manages all the events conducted by the committee. The Technical Secretary is responsible for all the technical events conducted by the society. The treasurer handles the finance of the committee. The society also comprises of other members are from 2nd and 3rd year.
